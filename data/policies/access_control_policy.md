# Access Control Policy

**Document ID:** POL-ACCESS-001  
**Control area:** Access Control  
**Mock owner:** Security Governance  
**Mock effective date:** 2026-01-15  
**Dataset status:** Mock evidence only

## Purpose

This mock policy defines access-control expectations used by the evidence-review lab.

## Requirements

1. All workforce users must use MFA for production and administrative systems.
2. Privileged access must be reviewed at least quarterly.
3. Access reviews must include a named reviewer, review timestamp, population reviewed, exceptions, and closure notes.
4. Terminated users must have access removed within one business day of the termination notice.
5. Break-glass accounts must be reviewed monthly and must have compensating controls documented.
6. Contractor accounts must have an owner and an expiration date.

## Evidence sufficiency rule

Access-control evidence is sufficient only when it includes identity, timestamp, reviewer, evidence source, and gap closure status.

## Boundary

This policy is mock readiness evidence for a lab. It does not prove production operating effectiveness or SOC 2 certification.
